We're Here To Help You Feel Better

Welcome to UMatter, a dedicated web application designed to promote the mental well-being of students. Our mission is to create a supportive and nurturing environment where students can thrive emotionally and academically. Understanding the challenges students face, we have developed a comprehensive platform to address their mental health needs.

What is UMatter?

UMatter is your personal mental wellness companion, tailored to support you through the ups and downs of student life. Our app provides a safe, confidential space to explore your thoughts and feelings, access professional support, and develop skills to manage stress, anxiety, and other mental health challenges.

Our Mission

At UMatter, our mission is to empower students to prioritize their mental health, build resilience, and achieve their full potential. We are committed to creating a supportive and stigma-free environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Join Us

Join UMatter today and find the support, resources, and tools you need for a confident and peaceful student life. Because at UMatter, your mental health matters.

FEATURES

Our Features & Services

ChatMate

ChatMate is your friendly companion for casual, supportive conversations. Whether you want to chat about your day or share your thoughts, ChatMate is here to listen and respond, ensuring you always have someone to talk to.

→

TuneStream

TuneStream is your ultimate music companion. Enjoy seamless music streaming, discover new artists, and create personalized playlists to match your mood. Your musical journey starts here.

→

CalmCorner

Calm Corner: Allows students to select from various moods (anxious/worried, angry, lonely, stressed/overwhelmed) and provides tailored content to help them understand and manage their emotions.

→

StressAssess

Assessment: Evaluates students' mental stress levels as low, moderate, or high through a series of questions, providing personalized content and resources to manage and alleviate their stress.
